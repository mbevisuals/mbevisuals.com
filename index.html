About

Hi! I'm passionate about turning science and tech into compelling visuals. I love talking to researchers, diving into their ideas, and turning those into animations that both look great and stay true to the science.

My engineering studies help me ask the right questions and understand the logic behind the process, which makes the creative part all the more exciting.

If you have something you'd like visualized, whether it's a complex process, an innovative product, or a research idea, I'd love to hear from you. Just reach out, and let's make something cool together.
